Our Story

Margins© was born in 2025 out of a deep passion for books and the stories they carry. Founded by Gabriel Berioni, a student of Brigham Young University–Idaho, this platform was envisioned as a digital home for readers who find meaning in the margins of their favorite books. Gabriel, a lifelong lover of literature, created Margins© as a way to bridge the gap between modern technology and the timeless act of reading.

The Idea Behind Margins

As a young reader, Gabriel often found himself writing notes in the margins of his books — reflections, emotions, questions, and quotes that resonated with him. Over time, these margins became more valuable than the printed text itself. Margins© was created to replicate that feeling in the digital world: a place where readers could preserve not only what they read, but how they felt while reading.

What We Do

Margins© is an interactive, user-friendly platform that allows users to search for books, mark them as read, write and store reviews, and rate their reading experiences. It’s a personal library, a notebook, and a community all in one. With features like book saving, rating systems, and review archiving, Margins helps users track their reading journey and keep their favorite insights in one place.

Why It Matters

We believe reading is not just about consuming content — it’s about transformation. Every book shapes who we are. In a time when digital distraction often replaces deep reading, Margins© invites users to slow down, reflect, and preserve the emotional weight of their reading experience. It fills a unique need for book lovers who crave more than just a list of titles — they want meaning, memory, and connection.

Looking Ahead

Margins© is just getting started. In the coming months, we plan to roll out new features including:

  • Social profiles and the ability to follow other readers
  • Comment sections for each book review
  • Reading goals and yearly summaries
  • A mobile-friendly experience and app integration

Our goal is to grow into a global reading network where each user has a voice, a bookshelf, and a story worth sharing.

A Personal Note from the Founder

“Books have always been a part of my life. They’ve helped me understand the world, escape from it, and come back to it with new eyes. Margins© is a dream come true — not just a project, but a piece of who I am. I hope this space can bring other readers the same joy, discovery, and companionship that books have brought to me.”
— Gabriel Berioni
